## ğŸ“‹ **Step-by-Step Guide**

### **ğŸƒ Create Your First Match**

#### **Step 1: Launch the App**
- Open Sideline Stats on your device
- Allow necessary permissions (notifications, storage)
- Grant camera access if prompted

#### **Step 2: Set Up Match**
- Tap the **"+"** button to create a new match
- Enter opponent name (e.g., "Eagles")
- Tap "Start Match" to begin tracking

#### **Track Events**
- **Goals**: Tap the goal button when your team scores
- **Penalties**: Use the penalty button for fouls
- **Cards**: Record yellow/red cards if applicable
- **Undo**: Swipe left on any event to remove it

#### **End Match**
- Tap **"Finish?"** when the game ends
- Choose your export method (email, CSV, or copy)
- Save your data before leaving the screen
